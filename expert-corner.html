How to Choose the Right Tiles

Choosing the right tiles depends on factors like durability, finish, and material. Ceramic tiles are great for walls, while porcelain tiles are ideal for high-traffic areas. Consider slip resistance for bathrooms and gloss level for aesthetics.

Tile Maintenance Tips

Regular cleaning with mild detergents keeps tiles looking new. Avoid harsh chemicals that erode the surface. Sealing grout lines prevents dirt buildup. For stubborn stains, use a mixture of vinegar and water.

Latest Trends in Tile Design

Hexagonal patterns, wood-look tiles, and large-format tiles are trending. Matte finishes offer a subtle, modern touch, while glossy tiles add brightness. Mixing textures can create unique interior styles.
